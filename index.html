Who We Are

We are a licensed, full-service electrical contracting company specializing in commercial work throughout the Omaha Metro. As a locally-owned and operated company, Wulff Electrical is the Contractor of choice for homeowners, building contractors, business owners, and property management companies.

Call us today, and experience what only the best Omaha area electrical contractor can provide.

For more information or a quote, please contact us at 402.498.9585.

Master Electrician Accredited
Master electricians are the best in their trade. They are very experienced and highly educated. At Wulff electric we have over 20,000 hours working and perfecting our craft as master electricians. We are always continually advancing our electrical engineering education to ensure we can continue to offer our customers the very best.
Lighting Trade Ally
Wullf Electric Inc. is a Lighting Trade Ally. Lighting Trade Allies provide no cost, no obligation lighting audits and proposals to OPPD customers. They have also attended training to ensure they are familiar with the Lighting Incentive Program, and all associated paperwork.
